Settings

You can change or update your information and settings at any point by selecting the setting menu item from the dropdown menu under your profile. Be sure to make sure any information that you not comfortable with sharing is hidden.

Ratings

Each Member has two types of rating. Advancing Human Rights gives each Member a rating based on the information they provided during the vetting process. You can see a users AHR rating by checking out their profile page. In addition each Member on the site can be rated by other Members. You can also see these ratings on the profile page, and we encourage you to rate users after you connect with them to help build up a trust network.

Staying Safe

We Recommend that for your own safety and security, users should not share personal contact information (such as your phone number, address, email address, etc.) with other users.
