Exchangivist is currently in beta

Exchangivist is currently in beta and has not undergone a full risk threat assessment.

Although we have done our best to ensure any information stored in Exchangivist is secure, we can not guarantee this until the first full version of the system is release later this year. Before you can sign up to Exchanivist you will need to go through a vetting system so Advancing Human Rights can confirm your identity. The same process is used for activists and people offering help.

No information other than you're username, email and either a hash of your password or data needed to authenticate your social login will be stored on the system until the vetting process is complete. The information you provide below will be sent to Advancing Human Rights in an email via a secure email service. This email will be deleted once the vetting process is complete.
