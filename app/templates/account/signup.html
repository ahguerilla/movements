Exchangivist is currently in beta

Exchangivist is currently in beta and has not undergone a full risk threat assessment.

Although we have done our best to ensure any information stored in Exchangivist is secure, we cannot guarantee this until the first full version of the system is released later this year. Before you can sign up to Exchanivist you will need to go through a vetting system so Advancing Human Rights/ Movements.org can confirm your identity. This process is used for anyone wanting to actively use the site.No information other than you're username, email and either a hash of your password or data needed to authenticate your social login will be stored on the system until the vetting process is complete. The information you provide below will be sent to Advancing Human Rights in an email via a secure email service. This email will be deleted once the vetting process is complete.

While Exchangivist, Movements.org and Advancing Human Rights have taken all possible steps to protect your privacy and security, there are still risks associated with your use of the Exchangivist platform. Your internet use may be tracked and traced back to you and AHR and its associated organizations cannot guarantee your safety. Additionally, Exchangivist is based on trust and we do our best to ensure that all community members are who they say they are and are participating with the best and most honest intentions. However we cannot guarantee the privacy, security, or anonymity of users.
