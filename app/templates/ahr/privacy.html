Welcome to Exchangivist.org.

Advancing Human Rights and Exchangivist.org is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website. When you visit Exchangivist.org the site automatically recognizes your domain name, IP address and the type of web browser you are using. Exchangivist.org does not acquire your e-mail address or other information without your knowledge. We use the information we collect as you browse our site to manage the site more efficiently and improve its content and functionality.

Cookies. The Exchangivist.org web server may ask your browser to store a “cookie,” which is a small piece of information that the web server uses to keep track of the use preferences. Cookies are not used to save or retrieve any sensitive data about visitors to the site.

Access to Registration Information. Exchangivist.org will under no circumstances give access to your registration information to any third party for any purpose, either commercial or otherwise. Your information is maintained on a secure web server in what we believe to be a well-protected environment.
