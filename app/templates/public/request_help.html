How to Request Help

 

If you'd like to make a request for help, click on MOVEMENTS at the top of your page to get to your dashboard. Click add post at the top right, and then click Request.

  • Indicate which skills you need.
  • Indicate in which regions or countries this help is needed.
  • In the first text entry field (What Do You Need), state what kind of help you need.
  • In the next box, describe the situation in more detail.
  • Indicate whether we can tweet your offer (we will not use personal information)
  • Click Post Now

Get started and request help now.
