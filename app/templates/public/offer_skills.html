How to Offer your Skills

 

To offer your skills, click on MOVEMENTS at the top of the page to get to your dashboard. Click Add Post at the top right of your page, and then click

  • Indicate which skills you have to offer.
  • Indicate which regions or countries you'd like to help (ALL is default).
  • In the first text entry field (How Can You Help) state what you are able to do and what help you can provide.
  • In then next box, describe what you can do in a little more detail.
  • Indicate whether we can tweet your offer (we will not use any personal information)
  • Click Post Now
