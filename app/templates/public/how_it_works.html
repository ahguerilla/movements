How it Works

It's simple. If you're an activist in need, or a concerned citizen who wants to help, signing up only takes a minute.

1) Create an account and fill in your profile. Provide however much information you are comfortable giving.

2) Post a request or an offer while being as specific as possible.

3) Search other requests and offers and connect with activists around the world.

4) Send a direct message or post a comment to connect with other users. Work together to help secure human rights and open closed societies.

Read more about how to offer your skills and how to request help.
